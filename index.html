Sijing (SJ) Shao is a current graduate student in quantitative psychology at the University of Notre Dame.

SJ's primary research interest falls in zero-inflated models in longitudinal data as well as in ordinal data. SJ has worked on serveral project on such topics including a power calculation package for ordinal data and a simulation of zero-inflated models. CV (PDF)»
